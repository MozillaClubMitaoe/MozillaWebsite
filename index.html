F.A.Q

  • Who are we?

    Mozilla Club MIT AOE is a technical club that introduces students to the world of open-source software and development and encourages them to solve problems using technology and innovation to make the world a better place. We are a community of students and enthusiasts who strive to make a difference in the experience of open web and open-source software by organizing various National and institute level technical events, workshops, and hackathons.
    You can know more about us here

  • What is the event about?

    IGNITE ENTREPRENEURS is a national event organized by Mozilla Club MITAOE that tries to discover and promote individuals with innovative entrepreneurship ideas that effectively require zero financial investment. 
    The participants will have to pitch their ideas through multiple rounds to ultimately make their idea stand as the best idea & win exciting prizes!

  • How to participate?

    You can participate in the event by filling Registration link and paying the nominal participation fee.

  • How much is the participation fee?

    Registration Fee for Individual 25 ₹ and for group of 2 is 50 ₹

  • Elimination rules?

    A participant will be eliminated if their idea cannot be initially implemented with zero or nominal investment.
    The idea presented by the participant should be practically implementable and the participant should have a thorough plan for it.

  • How many rounds will be there?

    2 Rounds as follows:

      1st Round questions related to startups. This will be an Elimination round where every participant has to give a small presentation of their idea💭 in front of a panel, explaining how their idea fulfills the criteria of zero investmentand how their ideas are unique and involve innovation🔡.

      2nd Round It will be the final round of the competition, where every participant needs to pitch their idea💡 to the judges. It will consist of
      1. Presentation of the Idea👨🏻💻💡
      2. Cross-Questioning from the judging panel👨🏼⚖️
      3. Final Grading from the panel on different criteria to declare the winner📈


  • How will the winner be selected?

    Participants with the highest score in the Final round will be selected as winners. Participants will be scored by the Judges based on multiple factors.

  • What would be the prizes?

    The winner would get a chance to win exciting cash prizes and also get a chance to win paid internships.

  • Will we get a certificate?

    Participants who clear the 1st (Elimination) round would receive certificates within 3 days of the event.
